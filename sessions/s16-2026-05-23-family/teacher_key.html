Note: Accept reasonable student classifications. The category boundary is pedagogically open here.
Complication Guide — Paso 4
Trigger: Student A says el suegro es estricto pero justo. Student B has no description of el suegro (Papá Roberto) from their cards.
Task: Both must negotiate and agree on exactly two adjectives for Papá Roberto. Both write the consensus on their cards.
Acceptable resolutions: Any two adjectives that both partners can justify. Likely: estricto y justo (most direct), or serio y respetado, or responsable y firme. The goal is negotiation, not a unique answer.
Intervention cue: If a pair stalls for more than 90 seconds, prompt: "¿Qué significa 'justo' para ti?" to restart the negotiation.
Grammar Focus Notes
Target 1 — Present tense of ser: Monitor for Mi madre es simpática (correct) vs. Mi madre está simpática (incorrect in this context — estar is not a target here). If students produce estar, note it but do not stop the interaction; address in post-task reflection.
Target 2 — Descriptive adjectives: Watch for gender agreement: la hermana es generosa (not generoso). The vocabulary set includes both -o/-a adjectives and invariable forms (inteligente, grande). Invariable adjectives are a useful teachable moment if errors arise.
Sentence frames in student materials: Mi [familiar] es [adjetivo]. and Su [familiar] es [adjetivo]. Both frames are in the student artifact; students must choose between mi (describing their own card) and su (recording what their partner said).
Success Criteria (Gate A, frozen SCB)
1. Students correctly use ser + adjective in Paso 2 and Paso 3 frames.
2. Students complete the information exchange without switching to English (Paso 3).
3. Each pair can identify and describe all 5 target family members assigned to both cards.
4. Both partners reach a consensus description in Paso 4 (negotiation documented in writing).
5. Pair presents a coherent 3–5 sentence family portrait in Paso 5 that references Paso 3 notes and Paso 4 consensus.
Differentiation Notes
Lower-proficiency students: Assign the role with more receptive support. Student B's cards require less production in Paso 2 (fewer family members to describe initially). Consider pairing a lower-proficiency student as Student B.
Extension: Early-finishing pairs can write a 3-sentence paragraph summary: "La familia [apellido] tiene [número] miembros. El [familiar] más interesante es ___ porque ___."
Time pressure (under 35 min): Cut Paso 5 class voting; preserve pair presentations only. Skip Paso 1 if extremely time-pressed — it is scaffolding, not the core gap.
