Paso 4 — Negotiate the Complication (Negocia la descripción)
Challenge / Desafío

Your partner says el suegro (Papá Roberto) is estricto pero justo. You have no other description of him from your cards. Together you must choose exactly two adjectives that you both agree describe Papá Roberto completely and accurately. You must both be satisfied — discuss and negotiate in Spanish!

Creo que el suegro es ___ porque ___.
¿Estás de acuerdo con ___?
